# How to Help

The fastest way to help right now is to tell me whether any of this is worth continuing.

There's no donate button on this page. There's no LLC, no Patreon, no Stripe account. Setting any of that up takes real effort, and I'd rather find out first whether there's enough interest to justify it.

So this page is a survey, not a checkout.

## The survey

If you've read at least one article on this blog and have an opinion, please answer a few quick questions.

- Found via search / link / social
- Recommended by someone
- Already follow my work elsewhere
- Other (tell me)

## How to submit

Open an issue on this repository titled `[Survey] <date>` and paste your answers. If you want to stay anonymous, you can omit identifying details — only the answers matter.

Direct contact options (email, sponsor links, etc.) will appear here once there's enough demand to justify formalizing them. Until then, repo issues are the channel.

## What happens next

- **If responses are sparse:** I keep writing whatever I find interesting, on my own schedule, no infrastructure changes.
- **If responses are strong and consistent:** I form an LLC, set up donation / sponsorship / loan infrastructure, and replace this page with actual links. The survey results will inform what gets prioritized first.

## Things that always help, no infrastructure required

- **Share an article** with someone who'd find it useful. Word of mouth is the entire growth strategy.
- **File an issue** with a factual correction, a counterargument, or a question that wasn't answered.
- **Open a PR** if you have a fix, a clarification, or a typo to flag.
- **Tell me what's wrong.** Especially the parts you disagree with. The articles get better when readers push back.

Thank you for reading.
